Welcome to **Infinity: Mercenaries**, a campaign framework for Infinity N5 where players take the role of a Captain in the harsh world of mercenary contracts.

Players create and develop a Company of battle-hardened veterans over the course of a season. The game is smaller than standard Infinity, using fewer units, but those units gain experience, new equipment, and grievous injuries through the campaign.

## Acronyms

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **CR** | Credits. Funds gained through contracts or special events. Spent on recruitment, equipment, and injury recovery. |
| **XP** | Experience Points. Earned by units during games and spent on personal development. |
| **SWC** | Special Weapon Cost. As in Infinity N5, this represents access to limited special weapons and resources. |

## Quick Start Reading Path

A long rulebook can be daunting. If you do not want to read the whole document up front, you can use this site as a reference and learn the details as you play.

At a minimum, players should read this introduction and then [The Company](/rules/the-company/) when creating a team.

Event organizers should expect to read the full rules, since players will rely on them for rules questions and event organization.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Overview" title="The Campaign Loop">
  <ol>
    <li>Create a Company from one or two sectorials, or one vanilla army.</li>
    <li>Choose a Captain and recruit the initial squad.</li>
    <li>Play contracts during a season.</li>
    <li>Gain XP, CR, injuries, renown, and notoriety.</li>
    <li>Use downtime to recruit, heal, buy equipment, and develop the Company.</li>
  </ol>
</RuleCard>
