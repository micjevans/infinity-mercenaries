## Credits

### Micjevans

Author of the current adapted Mercenaries ruleset and sole developer of this web app, micjevans has been working on the project since the start of 2022.

### Lareon

Author of the original Mercenaries concept and ruleset. We are very thankful for the brilliant foundation to build this project on top of. The original post that started the project can be found [on the Corvus Belli forums](https://forum.corvusbelli.com/threads/infinity-mercenaries-a-skirmish-campaign-framework-for-infinity-n4.38512/).

### Perzan

Long-time contributor to the Mercenaries project and rules, Perzan has provided consistent guidance to all members of the team.

### Bagelebagelebagele

The latest addition to the team, Bagele is a contributor to the Mercenaries project and rules and has already delivered fantastic concepts incubated in their local league in the form of the War Markets and an exciting Shipwrecks season.

### Drey

Former contributor and author to the Mercenaries project and rules. Drey worked actively and tirelessly on the project until early 2024.

### Sneaky Snake

Contributor to the Mercenaries project, rules, and art. Sneaky Snake always has creative and exciting ideas to spare.

### Corvus Belli

Developer and distributor of Infinity The Game. Mercenaries literally would not exist without the unique and wonderful rules they have created.

## Infinity Artists & Acknowledgement of AI Usage

We would like to be transparent about the fact that we utilize AI tools to speed up the development of this project and give credit to any of the wonderful artists that these tools may inadvertently rely on. We are a very small team of a few active individuals with full time jobs and none of us are competent artists. AI helps us to create a more professional project and a better experience for our players. If the project were to ever turn into something larger or generate revenue, we would love to have hand-crafted art from thinking and feeling artists.

Additionally, much of the artwork used in this rulebook has been found online and we could not trace all images to their creator. We would like to acknowledge and give credit to the amazing artists we could connect their art to. A special shout out to Corvus Belli and Modiphius Publishing artists, DNA-1, SENGHUI, chesterocampo, Aleksi Briclot, and AITUARMANAS.
