Before the first Contract and during Downtime, Companies have access to **2 markets**: the **Base Market** and one special **War Market**.

Players may select a War Market associated with one of their chosen factions or sectorials. After the first Contract, they may instead roll **1d20** to determine which War Market is available.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Random Market" title="Rolling a War Market">

Roll **1d20** and use the roll value listed on each War Market.

If a **19 or 20** is rolled, select any market. If the selected market is one the Company could already access because of its faction, reroll until the result is not a market they could have selected.

</RuleCard>

<RuleCard eyebrow="Notoriety" title="Market Alignment">

Each War Market has an Alignment that determines how Notoriety affects prices.

**Lawful** markets add the Company's Notoriety to item costs. **Chaotic** markets subtract the Company's Notoriety instead. **Neutral** markets are unaffected.

An item may only be discounted to a minimum of half its original cost, rounded up.

</RuleCard>
