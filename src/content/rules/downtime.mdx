## Downtime Events

Between Contracts, each Company resolves a single random event. Roll a **d20**, find the listed event, note any event Traits, then choose one of the available responses.

Each response lists a skill check in parentheses. After choosing a response and identifying the participating trooper, make the listed roll:

- A **pass** is equal to or lower than the target number.
- A **failure** is higher than the target number.
- A **critical pass** is rolling the exact number required.

Some Traits define who must participate, some define rewards or consequences, and some add special rules. It can look like a lot at first, but most Traits become easy to remember after a few events.

Services can also be purchased during Downtime. See the [Services](/rules/services/) reference page for the full list of paid one-time effects.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Example" title="Resolving a Downtime Event">

First, a 20 is rolled. The player chooses to reroll and gets a 5. That event has **MVP** and **Opponent (Mutual)** traits.

The player chooses the response "...do nothing and see what happens" and rolls against **PS=10**. They roll exactly 10, scoring a critical pass. The MVP of their last Contract gains **3 XP**, and because the event is mutual, the opponent's MVP also gains **3 XP**.

</RuleCard>
