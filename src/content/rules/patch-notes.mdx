Transparency and community involvement are crucial to the development of any product. This section provides a list of changes from the Mercenaries ruleset (v1.6) onward as well as notes the developers wish to add.

Changes are color-coded by type: <span style="color:#4caf7d">**green**</span> for additions and buffs, <span style="color:#c94040">**red**</span> for removals and nerfs, and <span style="color:#d4a840">**yellow**</span> for rewording and formatting changes.

<details class="patch-section" open>
<summary>Version m2-v1-3 → v2-0</summary>
<div class="patch-body">

<h3>General</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Version bumped to v2.0. Major additions including TAG Company and Loot Boxes warrant the upgrade.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Company — Company Types</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-remove">Deserter Company removed. NA2 sectorials are now valid, making it redundant.</li>
<li class="change-add">TAG Company added. Gains access to an AVA 1 TAG profile with additional benefits and downsides.</li>
<li class="change-add">Inspiring Leader added. Captain gains Inspiring Leadership for free; all other troopers must be Irregulars from any sectorial or vanilla.</li>
<li class="change-add">Airborne Company added. Captain gains Parachutist and Combat Jump; all troopers must be AD. Includes 1 free Speedball and allows AD during deployment.</li>
<li class="change-add">Proxy Pack added. One Lone Wolf deploys with several Aspects (modified netrods). The Lone Wolf can Transfer Consciousness between Aspects to play different profiles.</li>
<li class="change-add">Cohesive Company added. All members must be able to join a fireteam with the Captain; can form Harris and Core and always counts as pure.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Downtime — Perks</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Body: IMM (AP) moved to Tier 2; IMM (ARM/BTS) moved to Tier 3.</li>
<li class="change-remove">Empathy: Fireteam perks removed (now gained automatically on level up).</li>
<li class="change-add">Empathy: Discover +3 added as Tier 1; Discover (Reroll) added as Tier 2.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Equipment</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">STAT=# augments added across multiple markets, opening a wider range of builds.</li>
<li class="change-add">Number One Market: BS=11 augment added.</li>
<li class="change-add">Frontier General Market: PH=12 augment added; Immunity (IMM-B) added with BTS=0 restriction (cannot be raised above 0).</li>
<li class="change-add">Alpha Sec Market: WIP=12 augment added.</li>
<li class="change-add">Bantai Yamaco Market: CC=21 augment added.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Rule Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Banned Skills section added for clarity on what is prohibited.</li>
<li class="change-remove">BS Attack (T2/DA/EXP/Continuous): Banned. Applying these ammo types to all attacks is too powerful in Mercenaries.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Speedball and Transmutation adjustments moved to the Banned Skills section.</li>
<li class="change-add">Number 2: Now retains original benefits and can additionally reform for free.</li>
</ul>

<h3>New Rules</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Loot Boxes added. Randomly generated weapons that can exceed normal availability. Intended as a high-RNG reward at organizer discretion.</li>
</ul>

</div>
</details>

<details class="patch-section">
<summary>Version m2-v1-2 → v1-3</summary>
<div class="patch-body">

<h3>General</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Minor point cost adjustments, typo fixes, and wording clarifications.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Company — Company Types</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Reinforcements now count as their own distinct sectorial; can be taken alongside any other sectorial or reinforcements.</li>
<li class="change-remove">Reinforced Company removed (made redundant by the above change).</li>
</ul>

<h3>Playing a Season — Classified Objectives</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Classified Objectives added. Similar to vanilla Infinity classifieds but often provide buffs or rewards instead of OP. Always used unless stated otherwise; one selected, one randomly drawn.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Downtime — Ranking Up</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Free skills adjusted. Previous Rank 5 and 7 skills removed; replaced with fireteam perks at Rank 3 and 5. Warhorse and NCO devalued those skills late in the season; fireteam perks provide thematic bonding between mercs.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Perks</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Initiative: MOV progression changed to 6-2 → 6-4 → 6-6 at Tiers 1, 2, and 4.</li>
<li class="change-add">Body: Terrain (Total) added as Tier 1.</li>
<li class="change-add">Equipment: Reduced cost of deployable structures (armed turrets, cover, QAZ) to account for the baggage replenishment nerf.</li>
<li class="change-add">Exrah Exoskeleton renamed to "Unstable"; gains the Explode skill. Buffed due to previously poor performance.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Rule Adjustments — Modifications</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Journalist: Now adds or subtracts 1 Notoriety on deployment. Thematically fun; taxed by being on a weak unit.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Transmutation (X): Added note that Neurocinetics applies to both profiles as an exception.</li>
<li class="change-add">Number 2: Now allows forming fireteams for free.</li>
<li class="change-add">Peripherals: No longer required to be in a fireteam. Now treated as Irregular; controller gains FT Master skill.</li>
<li class="change-reword">NFB: Clarified how switching NFB equipment works when skills cannot be used together.</li>
</ul>

</div>
</details>

<details class="patch-section">
<summary>Version v1.6 → m2-v1-1/2</summary>
<div class="patch-body">

<h3>General</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Document style updated to align with Corvus Belli's Infinity aesthetic. Images and header added.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Resource Points renamed to Credits — more broadly understood and distinguishable from Renown.</li>
<li class="change-reword">New cover image on title page.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Company — Company Types</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">A single Vanilla army may now be taken instead of 2 sectorials.</li>
<li class="change-remove">Cohesive Company removed. The +1 WIP was too strong and discouraged WIP-based lists from taking multiple sectorials. May return with a different bonus in a future version.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Captain</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">+1 SWC now applies when your Captain provides +1 SWC, in line with base Infinity rules and the updated SWC system.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Recruitment — Peripherals</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-remove">Only 1 Peripheral may be purchased at a time per Peripheral owner.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Notoriety</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">New mechanic. Provides surcharges or discounts at markets. Gained primarily through downtime events. Measures how criminally notorious a company is.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Playing a Season — Sponsors</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Sponsors moved to Running a Season. Now function as organizer-chosen collective quests rather than individual XP grabs. Completing milestones grants sponsor benefits.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Contracts</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-reword">Elite Deployment: Reworded for clarity.</li>
<li class="change-add">CR and SWC Gain: Adjusted to 10 × Round Number (max 40). Removes snowball effect from previous Renown-based formula; works with Inducements to even out matchups.</li>
<li class="change-add">Bye Rounds: Updated for round-number CR formula; awards 3 OP worth of CR to soften byes.</li>
<li class="change-add">Inducements: Albedo -3 and -6 added.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Downtime</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Old Downtime tables replaced with a d20 event + skill-check system. More narrative, less gameable. Rewards and consequences are meaningful but not severe.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Disaster Tracker replaced by Notoriety.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Ranking Up: Veteran renamed to Warhorse (N5 change).</li>
<li class="change-add">Perks: Added option to roll for a perk (go one tier higher as a benefit).</li>
<li class="change-reword">Perks: All +1B changed to +1SD for a slightly more defensive game.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Perks: Climbing Plus moved to Tier 1 (nerfed in N5). Super Jump moved to Tier 3 (buffed in N5).</li>
<li class="change-add">Perks: Fireteam Duo added as Tier 3; Tier 4 allows counting as any other troop for bonuses.</li>
<li class="change-add">Perks: Doctor/Engineer +3 changed to ReRoll -3.</li>
<li class="change-add">Perks: Minelayer (2) added as Tier 2.</li>
<li class="change-reword">Perks: Natural Born Warrior moved to Tier 2 (N5 nerf). REM route buffed: Vita to STR at Tier 1, REM Plus/REM Pres at Tier 3.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Equipment</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Equipment cost influenced by Notoriety (discounts/surcharges per market).</li>
<li class="change-add">Companies now have inventories; equipment can be stored and transferred between troopers.</li>
<li class="change-add">SWC not spent on purchase — companies equip within their SWC cap. Equipment can be sold for half cost (rounded up).</li>
<li class="change-reword">Inventory, Loadout, and Purchasing and Selling sections added for clarity.</li>
<li class="change-add">Base Market replaces original equipment tables. Many items moved to War Markets; costs adjusted for N5.</li>
<li class="change-add">War Markets introduced. Randomly rolled per round; players access one market per round. All essentials remain in the Base Market so no roll feels mandatory.</li>
<li class="change-remove">Support Staff removed. To be redesigned; the downtime rework made the old system obsolete.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Services</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Anger Management: No Cover and Partial Cover are also lost if Impetuous is lost.</li>
<li class="change-add">Retirement: Retired troopers leave equipment in the inventory and do not add it to their Renown on departure.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Rule Adjustments — Modifications</h3>
<ul>
<li class="change-add">Fireteams: Only Duos allowed. Can be created with an Order instead of a Command Token.</li>
<li class="change-remove">Command Tokens: Speedball banned from Command Token usage.</li>
<li class="change-add">CC Attack: Troopers may apply any given CC PS to all their weapons (compensates for N5 removing PH from CC damage).</li>
<li class="change-reword">Bioimmunity removed (N5 now uses Immunity (ARM or BTS) natively).</li>
<li class="change-reword">Immunity Total removed (Corvus Belli updated N5 Immunity to match Mercs behavior).</li>
<li class="change-add">Peripherals: Reworked to act as normal troopers with restrictions/benefits. Gain XP and equipment; provide an Order. Some edge cases (e.g., camo Peripherals) accepted pending further balance observation.</li>
</ul>

</div>
</details>
