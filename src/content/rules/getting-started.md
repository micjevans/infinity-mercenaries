Infinity: Mercenaries is a campaign framework for playing smaller, narrative-driven games of Infinity N5.

## Core Idea

Players command persistent mercenary companies rather than one-off army lists. Operators earn experience, suffer injuries, collect credits, and return to the table with history.

## Campaign Loop

1. Build or update your company.
2. Select and play a contract.
3. Award XP, credits, and objective rewards.
4. Resolve injuries and downtime.
5. Update renown, notoriety, and roster state.

## Design Targets

- 4-7 units per player
- Four-round contracts
- Strong narrative consequences
- Meaningful between-game decisions
- Underdog support through inducements
