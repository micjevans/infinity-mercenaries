## New Recruits

The Company can be reinforced with new members during Downtime. CR gained from Contracts can be spent to buy new units and add them to the Company roster. Treat the unit's point value as its CR cost.

All rules listed in initial Recruitment apply to new recruits.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Recruitment" title="Roster Limits Still Apply">

Recruiting during Downtime does not bypass normal Company construction restrictions. Character, TAG, SWC-cost, AVA, and other recruitment restrictions continue to apply unless another rule specifically says otherwise.

</RuleCard>

## Retirement

Occasionally, it is time for a unit to leave the Company. A unit can be retired, freeing its roster slot for a new recruit.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Restriction" title="Captains Cannot Retire">

The Captain represents the focal point of the Company and cannot be retired in any way. They accompany the Company until the end of the campaign.
