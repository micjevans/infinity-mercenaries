## Season Length

All good things come to an end. The current Mercenaries ruleset is designed to support a Season of about **8 to 10 rounds**, and most events should end around that point.

Organizers may run shorter or longer events if it suits their local group, but the recommended default assumes Companies will have time to recruit, suffer injuries, gain XP, and develop meaningful identities over several Contracts.

## Win Conditions

To end a Season, determine the winner using a win condition chosen by the event organizer before the event begins.

The default recommended win condition is **highest Renown**.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Organizer Guidance" title="Choose the Ending Before the First Round">

Organizers are encouraged to use whatever win condition best captures the spirit of the event they have put together.

Highest Renown is the simplest default, but narrative leagues, escalation events, sponsor-driven campaigns, and one-day events may benefit from custom scoring or additional awards.

</RuleCard>

## Mid-Season N5 Updates

If Corvus Belli changes a unit profile during an ongoing Season, players may choose to keep using the old profile.

If a player wants to use the new profile and the new profile is more expensive, they must pay the additional CR cost. If the new profile is cheaper, the player may use the new profile but does not receive any CR refund.

Any new units purchased after the update must use the new profile.

## Closing the Event

At the end of the final round, organizers should collect each Company's final Renown and any event-specific scoring records. If retired troops are being tracked, include their recorded retired Renown according to the [retirement rules](/rules/services/#retirement).

After determining the winner, it is usually worth naming a few secondary recognitions as well: strongest narrative arc, most infamous Company, best underdog run, or favorite Captain. Mercenaries works best when the ending celebrates the season that happened, not only the number at the top of the standings.
