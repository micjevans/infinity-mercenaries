Loot Tables are used when rolling generated weapons from Loot Boxes. First roll the weapon base, then spend any remaining dice on weapon buffs.

For the full procedure, see the [Loot Boxes section](/rules/rule-adjustments/#loot-boxes) in Rule Adjustments.

<RuleCard eyebrow="Roll Procedure" title="Weapon First, Buffs After">

When rolling a Loot Box, choose how many dice to spend on the weapon base. Only the last die is rolled; the rest are treated as 6s.

After the weapon is generated, remaining dice may be spent on buffs using the same process until the Loot Box has no dice left.

</RuleCard>
