    <p>After every Order, add a Patience Marker to the Titan. When it has 5 Patience Markers, before the next Order is played, roll a d20.       <li><strong>Cull the Weak (1):</strong> all troopers with IMM-A or IMM-B make WIP-3. On failure they gain the IMM state they do not have; if they already have both, they are executed and removed.</li>

    </ul>
