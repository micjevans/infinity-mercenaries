    <p>When the Supply Box is first extracted, it is encrypted and must be decrypted twice using the Consoles. When it is extracted, the Personnel cap of each Sector immediately increases by 2.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Each Model can carry a maximum of 1 Supply Box. Troopers with Baggage can carry up to 2.</li>
      <li>Only Models, not Markers, can carry Supply Boxes.</li>
      <li>The Supply Box remains on the table even if its carrier enters a Null State.</li>
      <li>A Supply Box is controlled at the end of the game by a non-Null Model carrying it that is not in Silhouette contact with an enemy Model.</li>
    </ul>
